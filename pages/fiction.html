Project Prompt

“Nanogenerator,” which is able to harvest mechanical energy and transfer the energy into electricity, developed by a team from School of Material Science and Engineering. the team of nanognerator had developed four fundamental working modes to accommodate different types of mechanical motions. They also had applied vertical integration, proper selection of materials, and surface modifications to enhance output performance of nanogenerator for its potential application in the future.

Speculating

Based on current technology of nanogenerator, I speculate a future when this micro-generator is applied to industry and commercial market with a service called Power Bank. In my speculative future, energy is extremely expensive that even people from middle class would not have enough money to pay their electricity bills. But with the help of nanogenerators, which is prevalently installed on fitness equipments, we can generate electricity ourselves through transferring mechanical energy created by our motions into power. Every people has an account in Power Bank, which uses points to record amounts of power generated and amounts of power consumed. After depleting all points in your power account, you would have to pay for electricity or you would not have any power to use.

Power Bank Interface

On Power Bank online service, users can view account activities and status, power points checks, and purchase points products.
